What Is eLearning?

eLearning is a simple form of education that encourages learners to learn anywhere. It helps to gain complete knowledge with the help of the latest technologies. It is also understood as distance learning, online learning, or internet learning that suits everyone. Online learning is helpful for students and professionals who want to acquire diverse skills with customized learning. eLearning comes with various advantages for educators and learners, requiring only a computer or a mobile device and an internet connection. Anyone can buy a course that interests them and study it from their remote location. This has also enabled individuals to pursue, receive, and impart knowledge effectively at minimal expense.
